Game Overview

This game prototype is a first person shooter sci-fi roguelite. The player will upgrade and evolve their weaponry to survive waves of various enemies as they recieve bonus effects from leveling up.

Role, Responsibilities, and Tools

My role in this project was the technical designer responsible for the player's weapon upgrade and evolution system. This was created in Unreal engine 5 using the blueprint system.

Research Summary

This entry reflects the research topic that element interactivity reduced intrinsic cognitive load by supporting complexity of a mechanic through multiple interacting elements to lead up to the complex mechanic (Christine et al, 2024). This is implemented through the connected elements of upgrading aspects of a weapon interwoven with the aspect of being able to evolve the weapon into a new type (Unlikely Rogues, 2026). This can be improved by implementing the scaffolding approach by providing a tutorial explaining and exploring the system so that the player doesn't have to stumble through it (Chen et al, 2023).
